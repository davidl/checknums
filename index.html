Instructions

Quickly check Powerball tickets for a single drawing. Click the Add Ticket button and:

  1. Select 5 white balls and 1 red Powerball
  2. Click "Apply" to add your ticket and check it against the selected drawing at the top of the screen.
  3. Look at the top right corner of each ticket to see if you've won.
