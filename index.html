Instructions

Quickly check Powerball tickets for a single drawing. Click "Add Ticket" and:

  1. Select 5 white balls and 1 red Powerball
  2. Click "Check Ticket" to add your ticket and check it against the selected drawing at the top of the screen.
  3. Look at the top right corner of each ticket to see if you've won.

Selecting a drawing at the top of the screen checks all tickets, which are saved on your device until deleted.
